Style guide

Theme

The concept of my portfolio is to give a soft and modern feel.
I wanted to express neatness, and I made it simple by using two fonts.
I designed it not to be boring by properly mixing light and dark.
#컨셉 #부드러움 #모던함 #깔끔함 #밝음 #어두움 #믹스앤매치

Color

I wanted to create a modern and clean feeling
while giving a sense of stability.
The vanilla color of the nude tone was centered in dark gray,
and the orange color was added as a point.
